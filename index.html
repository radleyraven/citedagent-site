FAQ

Common questions.

How long until I see results?

Directory fixes are typically reflected in AI outputs within 2–6 weeks. Discovery visibility — appearing when buyers ask "best agent in [city]" — takes 8–12 weeks with consistent optimization.

Does this work for other professionals?

Yes — attorneys, doctors, and financial advisors are verticals we're expanding into. Real estate agents are our launch focus.

What if AI already knows who I am?

Most agents are findable by name but invisible for discovery queries — "best agent in [city]." Your Citation Score will show exactly where you stand on both dimensions.

Is this SEO?

No. SEO optimizes for Google search. Cited optimizes for AI model recommendations — a different data layer. The tactics overlap somewhat but the goal and measurement are entirely different.

What makes Cited different from a marketing agency?

We specialize in AI visibility specifically. Most marketing agencies are still optimizing for 2020 buyer behavior. We optimize for where buyers are going.
